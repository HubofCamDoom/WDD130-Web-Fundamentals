Content

Home page

One of the most important parts of my website will be to introduce myself and paint a professional picture of “Camden” to share with the world. The main content will essentially be a personal biography that tells of my life thus far and includes my experiences and education with the aim to sell myself to potential employers and interested individuals.

BIO - My name is Camden Asay. I am an information technology professional specializing in hardware and network tech support. Born and raised in Utah, I grew up enjoying the companionship of friends and family. I loved reading, especially anything that had to do with the mechanics of a contraption or vehicle. My favorite toy growing up was the wonderful building block system known as Legos. I would spend hours assembling and disassembling various creations, as I grew older, I was introduced to the technical side of my beloved plastic pieces, and my love for malleable complexities soared.

Tech transition - In pursuing continued satisfaction of my creative itch, I discovered the allure of video games. Here, in these fantastical worlds of make believe driven by the power of imagination and millions of lines of code, I found in myself an increasing desire to learn about how these came to be. I started attending school with the aim of obtaining a degree in information technology. I wanted to build and create, but I was limited in my understanding of the complexities that surrounded our wired world.

This page will also contain links to my contact information.
