Networking

Networking in IT refers to the practice of connecting computers and other devices to share resources, exchange data, and ensure efficient communication within and between organizations. It involves hardware components like routers, switches, and cables, as well as software and protocols that govern how data is transmitted and secured. A well-designed network supports business operations, enables collaboration, and provides access to critical services and information. As technology evolves, networking also plays a key role in cloud computing, cybersecurity, and the Internet of Things (IoT).

PC Hardware

Computer hardware technicians are professionals who install, maintain, and repair computer systems and related equipment. They work with physical components such as hard drives, motherboards, memory, and power supplies, diagnosing issues and replacing faulty parts to ensure systems run smoothly. These technicians may also upgrade hardware to improve performance and support new software requirements. Their role is essential in both business and personal computing environments, helping users minimize downtime and maintain reliable technology systems.
